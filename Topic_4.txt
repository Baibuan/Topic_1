The objective of this Convention and its protocols is to protect present and future generations from the devastating health, social, 
environmental and economic consequences of tobacco consumption and exposure to tobacco smoke by providing a framework for tobacco control 
measures to be implemented by the Parties at the national, regional and international levels in order to reduce continually and substantially 
the prevalence of tobacco use and exposure to tobacco smoke.
